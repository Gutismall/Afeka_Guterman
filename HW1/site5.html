Val Thorens, France

Val Thorens stands as the highest ski resort in Europe, offering exceptional snow conditions and access to the enormous 3 Vallées—one of the largest interconnected ski areas in the world. The resort sits in a bowl-shaped valley surrounded by towering peaks, creating a dramatic setting and ensuring long, snow-sure seasons. Skiers can enjoy everything from beginner slopes to high-alpine descents, efficient lift systems, and seamless connections to nearby Méribel and Courchevel. The village is modern, lively, and ski-in/ski-out, making it both convenient and full of energy. With its altitude, vast terrain, and vibrant atmosphere, Val Thorens is a favorite for skiers seeking both adventure and variety.

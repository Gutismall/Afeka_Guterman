Cortina d'Ampezzo, Italy

Cortina d'Ampezzo is one of the most elegant and storied ski destinations in the Italian Dolomites, renowned for its dramatic limestone peaks and timeless alpine charm. As the host of multiple Winter Olympics, Cortina combines world-class ski infrastructure with a distinctly Italian atmosphere full of style, fine dining, and boutique shopping. Its ski area offers a mix of wide open pistes, scenic cruising runs, and access to the vast Dolomiti Superski region. Whether you're exploring the breathtaking Cinque Torri area or admiring the pink-hued mountains at sunset, Cortina delivers a refined and unforgettable skiing experience that blends sport, culture, and natural beauty.

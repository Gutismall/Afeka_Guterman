Top 5 Must-See Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower: The iconic wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars.
  • Louvre Museum: Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa.
  • Notre Dame Cathedral: A stunning medieval Catholic cathedral on the Île de la Cité.
  • Arc de Triomphe: Honors those who fought and died for France in the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars.
  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica: A Roman Catholic church and minor basilica atop the Montmartre hill.

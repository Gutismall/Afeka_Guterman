Hong Kong

Asia's World City: Explore the vibrant blend of colonial history, modern skyscrapers, and natural beauty.

Top 5 Must-Visit Attractions

  • The Peak (Victoria Peak): Enjoy breathtaking views of the city skyline and harbor.
  • Star Ferry: Take the iconic, affordable ride across Victoria Harbour.
  • Tian Tan Buddha (Big Buddha): Visit the massive bronze statue on Lantau Island.
  • Tsim Sha Tsui Promenade: Perfect spot for the Symphony of Lights show.
  • Man Mo Temple: A serene and atmospheric traditional Chinese temple.
